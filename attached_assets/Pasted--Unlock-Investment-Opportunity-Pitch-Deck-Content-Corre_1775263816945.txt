# SLIDE 01 — The Problem

**Headline:** The Problem

**Subhead:** Most investors don't have a centralised, insightful view of their portfolio.

**Four problem statements:**

**1. Fragmentation**
Assets spread across platforms, wrappers, PDFs and people. The truth has to be rebuilt manually — whenever you need it, it isn't there.

**2. Adviser Gap**
IFAs can't advise on alternatives — gold, collectibles, EIS, crypto. The most interesting parts of your portfolio sit in a blind spot.

**3. No Operational Layers**
Platforms handle transactions. Nobody handles the operational reality: what you own, how it got there, and what it means.

**4. Tax as Reconstruction**
Year-end tax prep means reconstructing a year's worth of changes from scattered statements. Error-prone and expensive.

# SLIDE 03 — The Asset Register

**Headline:** The Asset Register

**Subhead:** Your single source of financial truth.

**Six features:**

**Lot-Level Tracking**
Every holding decomposed into original purchase lots — date, units, price. Cost basis evolution to current value.

**Snapshot Comparison**
Compare month-to-month or year-to-year. Separate market movement from your own actions.

**Document Linkage**
Statements, contract notes and valuations tied directly to the numbers they support.

**Income Visible**
Tracks every dividend, interest payment and coupon. Shows routing: reinvested, paid out, or held in custody.

**Household View**
One picture across people, wrappers, accounts and entities. Hidden exposures surface naturally.

**Plain Language Queries**
Ask questions in natural language: 'Show assets bought above £100K now worth less than £80K.'

# SLIDE 07 — The EIS & SEIS Opportunity

**Headline:** The EIS & SEIS Opportunity

**Subhead:** The UK's most powerful and most underused investment incentive.

**Four market statistics:**

| Stat | Figure |
|---|---|
| Invested through EIS/SEIS since inception | £34B+ |
| Invested in 2023/24 (EIS + SEIS combined) | £1.8B |
| UK businesses funded through the schemes | 59,000+ |
| UK unicorns that received EIS investment | 46.5% |

**Revolut: The EIS Success Story**
Revolut raised EIS-qualifying funding in its early stages. The company went on to become Europe's most valuable fintech — valued at $75B (November 2025). Early EIS investors received 30% income tax relief upfront. Their gains on exit? Entirely tax-free. This is what government-backed early-stage investing looks like when it works.

**Unlock's HNW Service**
Most investors know EIS/SEIS exists — but few have the tools to optimise it. Tracking eligibility across tax years, modelling allocation, and claiming reliefs properly still depends on expensive advisers. Unlock provides the platform. EIS/SEIS guidance is the high-net-worth service: tracking relief eligibility, modelling tax-efficient allocation across tax years, and ensuring nothing is left on the table. The register makes it possible. The guidance makes it valuable.

# SLIDE 15 — EIS & SEIS Tax Relief

**Headline:** EIS & SEIS Tax Relief

**Subhead:** UK government policy makes early-stage investing uniquely attractive.

**SEIS — Seed Enterprise Investment Scheme:**
- 50% income tax relief on investments up to £200K/year
- Capital gains tax exemption after 3 years
- Loss relief available

**EIS — Enterprise Investment Scheme:**
- 30% income tax relief on investments up to £1M/year
- CGT exemption and deferral available
- Loss relief available

**Worked example:**
Invest: £50K (SEIS).
Claim 50% relief = £25K back.
Your real cost: £25K.
If it exits successfully, profits are tax-free.
If it fails, loss relief cushions the downside.
Real risk: **~27.5p in the pound (at 45% additional rate).** ⚠️ CORRECTED from "~30p in the pound" — 30p applies at 40% higher rate; this example uses a 45% taxpayer.
