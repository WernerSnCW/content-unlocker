## PRODUCT GOALS (operator-supplied)

What this tool is for:
Unlock is an internal intelligence platform for a UK fintech company that manages investor relations at scale. It automates the process of analysing investor call transcripts, recommending the right compliance-approved documents to send, and generating new content using AI — all while enforcing strict regulatory compliance through Approved Content Units (ACUs) and a 19-check quality control system. It also tracks investor belief states, manages multi-channel campaigns, and detects content gaps across the document library.

Who uses it:
The primary user is an internal investor relations / content operations manager at the company. They are non-technical but highly domain-knowledgeable — they understand investor personas, pipeline stages, EIS/VCT tax relief products, and compliance requirements. They use the tool daily to decide what to send investors, generate compliant content, and maintain the document library.

What success looks like:

The recommendation engine accurately identifies investor persona, pipeline stage, and objections from call transcripts, then ranks the right documents to send.
Generated content always includes Locked ACUs and never includes Prohibited ACUs — compliance is non-negotiable.
The document library stays healthy: propagation cascades flag stale content, the work queue surfaces issues for resolution, and content gaps are identified before they cause problems.
The platform reduces manual effort — bulk imports, auto-generated email drafts, campaign asset generation, and PDF/Google Docs exports all work reliably.
Data integrity is maintained across leads, beliefs, documents, and campaigns with full audit logging.
What it is not for:

This is not a customer-facing tool — investors never see or interact with it.
It is not a general-purpose CRM — lead management exists only to support content targeting and recommendation.
It is not a document editor — content is generated by AI and reviewed, not manually authored within the tool.
It is not a marketing automation platform — campaigns are planned and generated here but actual delivery (email sending, ad placement) happens externally.
