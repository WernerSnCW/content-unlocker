## 2. TARGET CUSTOMER PROFILE

### Who We Serve

| Dimension | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Wealth range** | £1M–£20M+ total wealth (£250K–£5M investable focus for founding round) |
| **Age** | 55–75 primary, 45+ secondary |
| **Approach** | Self-directed or adviser-informed. NOT adviser-dependent. |
| **Portfolio** | Complex, multi-platform, multi-asset. Typically 5+ asset classes. |
| **Attitude** | Frustrated by fragmentation. Willing to pay for clarity. Interested in tax optimisation. |

### Who We Do NOT Serve
- Purely passive investors
- Retail mass-market (<£1M)
- Family office tier (>£20–50M)
- Adviser-dependent investors

### The Three Personas

| Persona | Motivation | Lead With | Key Language |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Preserver** | Protect wealth, minimise downside | Safety, validation, control | "peace of mind", "risk controls", "confirmed", "visible" |
| **The Growth Seeker** | Long-term wealth growth with calculated risk | Opportunity, transparency, access | "upside", "deal flow", "syndicates", "informed" |
| **The Legacy Builder** | Intergenerational wealth transfer, tax efficiency | Long-term, family, timing | "inheritance", "succession", "tax efficiency", "timing matters" |

### Persona Identification (First 3 Minutes of Call)

**Preserver signals:** Mentions risk, asks about downside, references adviser validation, mentions approaching retirement or recently retired.

**Growth Seeker signals:** Asks about deal flow, mentions EIS/SEIS before you do, asks about returns, references portfolio growth, mentions Seedrs/Crowdcube experience.

**Legacy Builder signals:** Mentions inheritance, estate planning, family, IHT, asks about BPR, mentions accountant or solicitor, references children/grandchildren.

**Red flags (not our customer):** Portfolio <£250K investable. Purely passive. Adviser-dependent (won't make decisions without IFA sign-off). Looking for guaranteed returns.

## 6. OBJECTION HANDLING

*All responses sourced from Content Bank Section 8. Do not invent responses outside this framework.*

### "I have an IFA"
IFAs can't advise on individual EIS company investments — only EIS funds and VCTs. Unlock fills the gap they legally can't. IFAs are structural partners. Unlock provides the intelligence tools and due diligence support that fill the advice gap.

### "I'm already diversified"
Most investors think they're diversified but hold correlated assets. Unlock shows what's actually happening across the full picture. Even pensions can be correlated. If markets fall 30%, fragmented investors are worst hit.

### "Too early-stage for me"
EIS relief means you're risking ~38.5p on the pound at additional rate — or ~27.5p under SEIS. The government underwrites the downside. Subject to individual tax circumstances and adviser confirmation. Loss relief is personal to the original investor and cannot be transferred.

### "I'll wait and see"
Founding terms — valuation, lifetime access, priority deal flow — are never offered again. Every month you wait is a higher entry price. April 6, 2026 share issuance is a fixed date.

### "I don't have time"
Set it up once. Monthly insights. No daily management. One platform, one view. White-glove onboarding for founding investors — the team does the setup.

## 7. CALL FRAMEWORK

### Discovery Call Structure (15 minutes)

| Section | Duration | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Opening** | 1 min | Personal introduction. One sentence: what Unlock does. |
| **2. Pain identification** | 3 min | Ask about portfolio fragmentation, tax management, adviser relationship. Listen for persona signals. |
| **3. Persona routing** | 1 min | Internal: classify as Preserver / Growth Seeker / Legacy Builder based on signals. |
| **4. Fit qualification** | 2 min | Portfolio size? Investable capital? Self-directed or adviser-informed? |
| **5. Problem confirmation** | 2 min | Reflect pain back in their language. "So you're saying..." |
| **6. Authority establishment** | 2 min | Founding backer credentials. SEIS assurance. 40+ market interviews. |
| **7. Value proposition** | 2 min | Persona-specific VP. Not features — outcomes. |
| **8. Next step** | 2 min | Book the demo. "I'll send you [persona-specific document] ahead of our meeting." |

### Demo Structure (15 minutes max)

| Module | Duration | Show |
|---|---|---|
| Asset Register | 2 min | Complete portfolio view — data foundation |
| Tax Intelligence Engine | 2 min | Live tax position. Show IHT change impact. |
| Portfolio Simulator | 2 min | Run a 2008-style scenario on demo portfolio |
| Decumulation Planner | 2 min | If Preserver or Legacy Builder |
| Unlock Access | 2 min | If Growth Seeker or Legacy Builder |
| Q&A + next step | 5 min | Answer questions. Send Pack 1. |

**Total: 15 minutes maximum. Do not run long.** Sophisticated investors' time is valuable. A demo that runs over signals poor preparation.
